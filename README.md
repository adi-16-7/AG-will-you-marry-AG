# AG, will you marry AG? 💍
### Adisha — a scrapbook for Akansha, made by Aditya

A chaptered, scrapbook-style proposal site. It works **right now** with elegant
placeholders, and gets even more beautiful once you drop your photos in.

---

## 1 · Add your photos

Put your photos in this `photos/` folder using these **exact file names**
(`.jpg` — lowercase). Any photo missing simply shows a pretty placeholder, so
add as many or as few as you like.

| File name | What goes there |
|---|---|
| `cover.jpg` | Your single favourite photo of you two (the cover) |
| `met-1.jpg`, `met-2.jpg` | Early college / "just friends" days |
| `nov17.jpg` | Something from your anniversary / where it began |
| `bangalore-1.jpg`, `bangalore-2.jpg` | Bangalore, living together |
| `her-1.jpg` | A candid of Akansha you love |
| `us-1.jpg` … `us-9.jpg` | The "wall of us" collage (your best 9) |

> Tip: `us-1.jpg` and `us-2.jpg` are the two **wide** banner photos in the
> collage — pick landscape (horizontal) shots for those two.

You have ~30 photos, so feel free to swap in your favourites for each slot.

## 2 · (Optional) Add a song

Drop an mp3 at `music/song.mp3`. The little ♪ button (top-right) lets Akansha
play it. It stays off until she taps it (browsers block autoplay), and swells
in when she says yes.

## 3 · Preview locally

Just open `index.html` in your browser. That's it.

## 4 · Deploy to GitHub Pages

1. Create a new repo named **`AG-will-you-marry-AG`**.
2. Upload everything in this `proposal-site/` folder to the repo root
   (so `index.html` sits at the top level).
3. Repo → **Settings → Pages** → Source: **Deploy from a branch** →
   Branch: `main`, folder: `/ (root)` → **Save**.
4. Wait ~1 minute. Your site goes live at:
   `https://<your-username>.github.io/AG-will-you-marry-AG/`

Send her the link. Maybe in the evening. Maybe on a 17th. 🤍
